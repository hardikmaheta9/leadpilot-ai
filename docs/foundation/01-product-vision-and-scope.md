# LeadPilot AI – Product Vision & Scope

## 1. Product Name

Working Name: LeadPilot AI  
Company: WebApp Infoway  
Product Type: AI-powered lead generation, CRM, and business development platform.

## 2. Product Vision

LeadPilot AI will help IT service companies, software agencies, freelancers, and consultants find, analyze, 
prioritize, and convert potential clients using automation and artificial intelligence.

The platform will not only collect leads but will help users understand:

- Which companies are most likely to need IT services.
- Why they are good prospects.
- Who should be contacted.
- What message should be sent.
- What the estimated project value could be.
- When follow-up should happen.

## 3. Main Problem

IT service companies struggle to find fresh, qualified, high-intent leads consistently.

Most businesses rely on:

- Manual LinkedIn search
- Cold emails
- Referrals
- Freelance platforms
- Paid lead lists
- Random outreach

These methods are time-consuming, inconsistent, and often low quality.

## 4. Proposed Solution

LeadPilot AI will automatically discover potential business opportunities from multiple sources, enrich company 
data, score leads using AI, and help users contact prospects with personalized outreach.

## 5. Target Users

### Primary Users

- Web development companies
- Mobile app development companies
- AI development companies
- Digital marketing agencies
- UI/UX agencies
- Software consultants
- Freelancers
- SaaS development companies

### Future Users

- Real estate consultants
- Legal firms
- Healthcare consultants
- Recruitment agencies
- Manufacturing consultants
- Financial service providers

## 6. Core Value Proposition

LeadPilot AI works like a virtual business development assistant.

It helps users:

- Find new leads daily
- Identify high-value opportunities
- Analyze company websites
- Generate personalized emails
- Manage CRM follow-ups
- Track deal pipeline
- Improve client acquisition

## 7. Version 1 Goal

The first version will focus on helping WebApp Infoway generate qualified IT service leads.

### Version 1 Must-Have Features

- Login system
- Dashboard
- Company management
- Contact management
- Lead management
- Lead sources
- Keyword management
- Lead scoring
- Notes and follow-ups
- Daily lead email report
- Basic AI email suggestion
- Settings panel

## 8. Future SaaS Goal

The product should be designed so it can later become a SaaS platform where multiple companies can create 
accounts and manage their own leads, campaigns, users, and settings.

## 9. Product Positioning

LeadPilot AI is not just a CRM.

It is a lead intelligence and business development platform for service-based companies.

## 10. Success Metrics

The product will be successful if it helps users:

- Find fresh leads daily
- Reduce manual prospecting time
- Improve outreach quality
- Increase meeting bookings
- Improve proposal conversion
- Generate more revenue

## 11. Initial Business Use Case

WebApp Infoway will use LeadPilot AI to find clients for:

- Website design
- Website development
- Mobile app development
- Graphic design
- AI automation
- Custom software development
- CRM/ERP development
- Laravel/PHP development
- WordPress development
- Shopify development

## 12. Development Philosophy

The product will be built with:

- Security first
- Clean architecture
- Modular design
- Scalable database
- Git version control
- Production-ready coding standards
- Future SaaS support
